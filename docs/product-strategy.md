# SmartTailor Product Strategy

## Positioning

SmartTailor is the operating system and marketplace for local tailoring: digital measurements, online orders, delivery tracking, payments, reminders, and reviews.

## Target Customers

- Customers who stitch ethnicwear, blouses, suits, uniforms, formalwear, and alterations.
- Local boutique tailors, alteration shops, home-based tailors, designer studios.
- Early niche: women's ethnicwear and blouse stitching in one city.

## MVP Scope

Phase 1 focuses on the workflow that creates daily value:

- Auth and role-based dashboards.
- Tailor profile.
- Customer measurements.
- Order creation and order status tracking.
- Image uploads.
- Razorpay payment records.
- Notifications.
- Reviews.

## Revenue Model

- Tailor subscription: INR 499 to INR 1,999 per month.
- Marketplace commission: 5% to 15% per order.
- Premium tailor listings.
- Enterprise uniform plans.
- AI and WhatsApp automation add-ons.
